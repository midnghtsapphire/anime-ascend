# Agent Handoff Checklist

## Comprehensive Agent Handoff Checklist

1. **Current Status Overview:**  
   - Review the current status of ongoing projects.
   - Identify any open issues or blockers.

2. **Knowledge Transfer:**  
   - Schedule a meeting for knowledge transfer sessions.  
   - Create a shared document for FAQs and key contacts.

3. **Documentation Review:**  
   - Ensure all project documentation is updated and accessible.
   - Highlight changes made since the last handoff.

4. **Tools and Access:**  
   - Verify access to necessary tools and accounts.
   - List any tool-specific credentials or access protocols.

5. **Team Introduction:**  
   - Introduce the new agent to the team members.
   - Include roles, responsibilities, and how to contact each member.

6. **Client Expectations:**  
   - Outline current client expectations and deliverables.
   - Provide any relevant client feedback or communications.

7. **Improvement Recommendations:**  
   - Document any areas for improvement identified during the review.
   - Suggest potential strategies or tools to enhance workflow.

## Documentation Review
- Ensure all relevant documentation is easily accessible and up-to-date.  
- Recommend updates where information may be lacking or unclear.

## Improvement Recommendations
- Always seek feedback on the handoff process itself to ensure it is as effective as possible.
- Regularly schedule reviews of the handoff materials to ensure they evolve with team needs and project goals.
